HEALTH

" Health encompasses the overall well-being of individuals and communities, focusing on physical, mental, and social aspects. It includes the study of diseases, medical treatments, preventive care, and public health initiatives aimed at improving quality of life. "

LIFE SCIENCES

"Life sciences involve the study of living organisms and life processes. This field includes biology, botany, zoology, microbiology, genetics, and ecology. Researchers in life sciences work to understand the complexities of life, from cellular functions to ecosystems."

PHYSICAL SCIENCES & ENGINEERING

" Physical sciences explore non-living systems and include disciplines like physics, chemistry, astronomy, and earth sciences. Engineering applies scientific principles to design, build, and maintain structures, machines, and systems. This field is crucial for technological advancements and solving practical problems."

SOCIAL SCIENCE & HUMANITIES

"Social sciences study human behavior and societies, covering areas like psychology, sociology, anthropology, economics, and political science. Humanities focus on human culture, including literature, history, philosophy, and the arts. Both fields aim to understand and interpret human experiences and societal developments. "
